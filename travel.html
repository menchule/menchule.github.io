Travel & cultural curiosity

Exploring other cultures is more than a personal passion — it is a professional asset. Travel sharpens my international mindset, improves how I collaborate across borders, and helps me understand how context shapes decisions, markets, and people.

Why it matters

Working in international environments requires more than language skills. It demands cultural awareness, adaptability, and the ability to read between the lines. Experiencing different countries and ways of living has trained me to navigate complexity with empathy and perspective.

Beyond work

I am naturally curious about how people live, create, and connect. From food and music to daily routines and social dynamics, travel continuously feeds my creativity and my way of thinking — both personally and professionally.
